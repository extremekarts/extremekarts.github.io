Facilities

Extreme Indoor Karts offer a variety of catering options from a casual BYO food and self cook BBQ, to a catered function working with top local caterers.

Self-cook BBQ: We have tables and chairs available along with two gas BBQ's which can be hired for $20. This covers rubbish disposal and gas for the BBQ's. You are welcome to bring your own food and drinks, or if you prefer ordering food the two options below will be perfect.

Hell Pizza: We are working along side Hell Pizza Manukau who have provided us with a fantastic deal to offer to our clients.
1. Free delivery on any pizza order regardless of how many pizza's you order (yes thats right, even for 1 pizza its free delivery!)
2. If you order 6 or more pizza's you will receive 3 free sides (these include garlic bread or wedges) ontop of your free delivery. You are welcome to order a combination of these as well.

Contact Us if you are interested in this special deal.

Kiwi King: We are also working along side a spit roast catering company just down the road from us. Please feel free to contact them in regards to their menu's and costs.
