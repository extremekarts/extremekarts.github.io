About us

Extreme is New Zealand's only two level Go Kart Track!
We cater for small and large groups, specialising in corporate events, stag parties and casual groups
Our location is close to the southern motorway meaning we are easily accessible from all directions

Extreme Indoor Karts has been running for over 20 years from the same location. Our experienced and knowledgeable staff will be able to assist you with the perfect race format for your group

We abide by all safety regulations and are inspected regularly by certified engineers and Worksafe to ensure we provide a safe operation. This extends from the track to the karts and customer safety.
