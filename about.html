About us

Extreme Indoor karts is New Zealands ONLY TWO LEVEL GO KART TRACK! If you havent tried it out yet you definately need to come along and give it a go. Its awesome fun and something totally different! With the recent opening of the Highbrook Motorway Offramp means we are now only 10 minutes from Aucklands CBD.

Extreme Indoor Karts has been running for over 12 years from the same premises and has just recently changed hands. The new owners bring experience in a broad range of professions, from marketing to engineering and have a combined experience running Extreme Indoor Karts of over 16 years.

We abide by all compulsory safety regulations as laid out by OSH. We have been inspected by OSH engineers to certify that we run a safe operation and that we are obiding by these regulations. This extends from the track to the karts to customer safety.
