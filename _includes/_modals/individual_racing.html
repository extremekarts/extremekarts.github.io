Individual racing

Individual racing is aimed at smaller groups who are looking at doing a fun and competitive event. These races are much more of a structured event than the casual racing. You have a choice of several different race setups (please see below).

Total booking time includes:

  • Signing In
  • Race Briefing
  • Driver Practice
  • The Race
  • Prize Giving

After each race drivers are given points depending on their finishing position, the winning driver getting 1 point and the loosing driver getting either 8, 9 or 10 points depending on how many drivers you have in total (last position = number of points assigned). On completion of all races a total is obtained and an overall winner is determined who then receives a prize from us at Extreme!

Important information

  1. Bookings are essential as your group will have the track exclusively to yourself.
  2. $150 non-refundable deposit is required 1 week in advance (2 weeks during November, December and January) - this can be refunded to you on the day (only if you turn up for the race you were booked in for) or you can use it towards the race.

Deposit can be paid by the following methods:

  • Cash
  • Eftpos
  • Credit Card (we are also able to take credit card payments over the phone)
  • Company cheques only (no personal cheques are accepted)
  • Direct credit

Minimum drivers required: 8

Maximum drivers required: 10
