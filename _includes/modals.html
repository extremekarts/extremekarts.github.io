Teams racing

Teams racing is aimed at medium to large groups who are looking at doing a fun and competitive event. These races are much more of a structured event than the Arrive & Drive. You have a choice of several different race setups (please see below).

As drivers will be driving in teams we generally aim to do driver changes every 15 or 20 laps depending on the race length and number of drivers in each team. This setup usually gives each driver a minimum of two sessions on the track.

Total booking time includes:

  • Signing In
  • Race Briefing
  • Driver Practice
  • The Race
  • Prize Giving

After the race results are presented and the winning team gets a prize from us at Extreme!

Important information

  1. Bookings are essential as your group will have the track exclusively to yourself.
  2. $150 non-refundable deposit is required 1 week in advance (2 weeks during November, December and January) - this can be refunded to you on the day (only if you turn up for the race you were booked in for) or you can use it towards the race.

Deposit can be paid by the following methods:

  • Cash
  • Eftpos
  • Credit Card (we are also able to take credit card payments over the phone)
  • Company cheques only (no personal cheques are accepted)
  • Direct credit

One hour track hire

Our track can also be hired on an hourly basis. Apart from racing, this is ideal for promotional events, product launches or even shooting music videos!

There is no minimum number of people needed for this event. A maximum of 60 people can be catered for.
